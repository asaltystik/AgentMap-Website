OR-Birthday Rule

Annual Open Enrollment lasting 60 days, beginning 30 days before
and ending 30 days after the individual's birthday, during which time
a person may replace any standardized Medicare supplement policy
with a policy of equal or lesser benefits. Coverage will not be made
effective prior to 30 days before the individual's birthday or beyond
60 days from the application date.

Law requires full compensation from every carrier.
Full comp paid on all ages
Manhattan Specific Rule - Can enroll up to 60 days before birthday. Latest
effective date can be is first of the month following the end of the 30-day period
