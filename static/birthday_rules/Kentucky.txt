KY-Birthday Rule

Annual open enrollment period lasting 60 days, beginning on an
individual’s birthday. The individual may purchase the same Medicare
supplement plan they currently have with a different insurer. Coverage
will not be made effective prior to the individual’s birthday or beyond
60 days from the application date.
