MD-Birthday Rule

Annual Open Enrollment lasting 30 days, beginning on an individual's
birthday, ending 30 days from that date, during which time an
individual may replace any Medicare supplement policy with a policy
of equal or lesser benefits. Coverage will not be made effective prior
to the individual's birthday or beyond 60 days from the application
date.

Lifeshield is only carrier paying full commission.
Lifeshield requires proof of coverage AND paid to date.
